About Us

About TrustForce Advisors

TrustForce Advisors was created to provide reliable, ethical, and organized fiduciary support for individuals and families navigating conservatorships, guardianships, trusts, and estate-related responsibilities.

Our mission is to protect vulnerable individuals, support responsible decision-making, and provide peace of mind through clear communication, accurate records, and compassionate service.

Meet DC McCraney

TrustForce Advisors was founded by DC McCraney to provide families, fiduciaries, beneficiaries, veterans, and professionals with organized support during some of life's most important transitions.

DC McCraney brings a multidisciplinary background in law, dispute resolution, project management, technology, and client advocacy. TrustForce Advisors was built on the belief that fiduciary responsibilities should be handled with integrity, transparency, accountability, and care.

Our Process

The TrustForce Process

A clear, structured path from first call to ongoing support.

01

Schedule a Consultation

Clients contact TrustForce Advisors to discuss their situation and service needs.

02

Needs Assessment

TrustForce Advisors reviews the type of support needed, including documents, deadlines, responsibilities, and goals.

03

Document Organization

Important records, account information, court documents, trust documents, or fiduciary materials are organized.

04

Support Plan

A clear support plan is developed based on the client's needs and responsibilities.

05

Ongoing Guidance & Reporting

TrustForce Advisors provides ongoing assistance, organization, communication support, and reporting preparation as needed.
